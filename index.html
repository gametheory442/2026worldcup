🏆 Scoring

Phase 1 — Group Stage

Exact group finishing position+3
Correct advance / eliminate tier+1
Tournament champion (pre-tournament pick)+15

Example: you pick a team to finish 2nd but they finish 3rd — if they still advance as a best 3rd-place qualifier, you get +1 (right tier, wrong exact spot, 0 if they're eliminated instead).

Phase 2 — Knockout Stage

Correct Round of 32 winner+2
Correct Round of 16 winner+6
Correct Quarterfinal winner+10
Correct Semifinal winner+14
Correct Final winner+20

Top 2 per group always advance · 4th is always eliminated · 3rd-place advancement depends on your Phase 1 qualifier picks. The champion pick bonus only counts if you made the pick before June 11.
